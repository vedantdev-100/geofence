⚠ GPS Accuracy & Bounding Box Logic
• Outdoor GPS accuracy: ±3–10 m (some inaccuracy is expected)
• Indoor GPS is often unreliable — buildings block satellite signals
• Set buffer ≥ your expected GPS error to avoid false rejections
• Bounding box uses the Haversine offset formula from anchor point
• 1° lat ≈ 111,320 m  |  1° lng ≈ 111,320 × cos(lat) m
• For room-level precision: consider BLE, UWB, or Wi-Fi RTT
